## üèó Core NLP Tasks
This project implements and explains several fundamental architectural tasks required to process human language effectively:

* **Tokenization & Normalization**: The process of segmenting continuous text into discrete units (tokens) and standardizing them (lowercasing, lemmatization, etc.) to facilitate machine comprehension.
* **Named Entity Recognition (NER)**: Utilizing statistical models to identify and categorize key entities within a text, such as corporate organizations, chronological dates, or specific medical nomenclature.
* **Sentiment Analysis**: Employing computational linguistics to decipher and quantify the emotional tone and subjective intent embedded within the text.
* **Syntax & Dependency Parsing**: Analyzing the underlying grammatical structure and the functional relationships between individual words to understand sentence logic.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Challenges in Raw Text Processing
Working with raw, "wild" text involves significant technical hurdles. This repository provides advanced strategies to mitigate the following:

* **Ambiguity**: Resolving polysemy and homonymy where words carry divergent meanings depending on their specific linguistic context.
* **Slang and Noise**: Implementing robust preprocessing pipelines to clean data harvested from social media or informal communication channels.
* **Structural Diversity**: Engineering solutions to manage heterogeneous document formats and non-standard syntax across varying data sources.
